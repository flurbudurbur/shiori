Why Bookmark?

🔒

Truly Anonymous

No accounts, no email, no PII. Your sync data is linked only to an anonymous bookmark ID that you control.

🔄

Seamless Sync

Keep your bookmarks, history, and settings consistent across all your browsers and devices effortlessly.

🗑️

Data Control

Your data is automatically deleted after 60 days of inactivity. You hold the key (your bookmark ID).

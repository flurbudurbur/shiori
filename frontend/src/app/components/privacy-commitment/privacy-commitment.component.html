Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Bookmark is built from the ground up with privacy as the core principle. We believe you should control your data, not us.

Anonymous by Design

We use anonymous bookmark IDs. We never ask for your email, name, or any other personally identifiable information. Your identity remains unknown.

Ephemeral Data

Your synced data (bookmarks, history, etc.) is automatically and permanently deleted from our servers after 60 days of inactivity associated with your bookmark ID.

No Tracking, No Ads

We don't use third-party trackers or analytics. We don't sell your data. Our focus is solely on providing a secure sync service.
